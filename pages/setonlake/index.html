Seton Lake In British Columbia

Location Seton Lake
Model Natan Antolin
Product Em Socks
Date 12/02/2018

We were on the hunt for this beautiful place on a trip to Whistler. Initially, we weren't going to make it due to the distance. Luckily(?), I took a pretty hard fall on my head and decided not hitting the slope on the second day.

The drive there from Whistler was absolutly stunning. The road was so twisted makes me want to come back with my motorcycle again. Although, It took us about 2 hours just from Whistler to Squamish due to some accident. Even though we were dying on the way back, it was still worth it. BC never disappoints.
